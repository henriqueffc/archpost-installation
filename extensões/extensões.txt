# Extensões do GNOME:

Recomendo utilizar o programa Extension Manager - https://flathub.org/apps/details/com.mattjakeman.ExtensionManager (instalado pelo scrpit de instalação número 4) para instalar as extensões. 

AppIndicator and KStatusNotifierItem Support - https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/615/appindicator-support/

Gnome Clipboard History - https://github.com/SUPERCILEX/gnome-clipboard-history

No overview at start-up - https://extensions.gnome.org//extension/4099/no-overview/

Sound Input & Output Device Chooser - https://extensions.gnome.org//extension/906/sound-output-device-chooser/

Vitals - https://extensions.gnome.org//extension/1460/vitals/
